How Does it Work?


  • Apply for the next cohort

    Fill and submit the application form.Please note that there is an application fee attached.

  • Write an assessment test

    After a successful application, you will be asked to write an assessment test. Pass the test, and get shortlisted to join the program.

  • Join the program

    Join the program and begin your journey into the Blockchain world. For 9 months, we will train you, and give you the tools to compete on a global level.

  • Pay your tuition fee

    After learning and graduating, we will help you with job placements. You would only pay for your fee when you get a job. The payments will be via an income sharing agreement, and split into small parts until you’re done paying.

What's Our Mission?

Create Awareness

Blockchain is opening up new worlds in the technology space, and it’s still in its early days. Being part of the new world will create a level playing field for Africans to compete globally. We want to make sure Africans won’t miss this train.

Access Education

Accessing quality education and getting all the resources to excel comes at a massive cost that most people cannot afford. We wanted to change that; that’s why we decided to provide world-class education and resources to everyone for FREE.

Create Jobs

One of the ways we can improve lives and the economy in Africa is to create high paying jobs. That’s why we’re on a mission to reduce entry barriers and give people the opportunities to earn a better living in the rapidly growing Blockchain industry.
